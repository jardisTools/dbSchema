## License Scope

A commercial license is granted to **one legal entity only**.

- Each legal entity (GmbH, AG, Ltd., Inc., etc.) requires its own license
- Subsidiaries, parent companies, and affiliated companies are NOT covered
- Joint ventures and partnerships require separate licenses
- Licenses are non-transferable between legal entities

**Example:** If "Acme GmbH" holds a license, this does NOT extend to "Acme Holding AG" or "Acme Solutions GmbH" - each requires a separate license.
